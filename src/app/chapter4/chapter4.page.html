Chapter 4 | Alita Alita

Alita is my domestic short hair. She was born May 6, 2019 outside of Salinas, CA. Alita was rescued from the engine compartment of a fire truck! Four firemen escorted her into my vet's office when she was about six weeks old. She was meant to be mine. Alita is the most affectionate cat; she loves to cuddle and insists on sleeping on my chest every night.
